Oh no! A lab experiment has gone wrong, bringing the dead back to life, turning them into mindless zombies!
Fortunately, Mike, a military veteran was given a gun with special bullets made by the scientists to kill the undead is tasked with going around town shooting the zombies to send them back to their graves! 
Can Mike kill all the zombies before they get to him first> It's up to YOU to control Mike so that he can kill all the zombies!

Press the right and left arrow keys to move

Press the up arrow key to jump

Press the spacebar to shoot

The zombies are fast, so you have to have quick reflexes to avoid their attacks by jumping over their heads.

The zombies take 3 bullets to kill each

If Mike gets hit 2 times, he dies, but the zombie infection turns HIM into a zombie to join the apocalypse.
